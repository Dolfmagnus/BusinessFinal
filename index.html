Our Promise

Every Piece
Has a Past Life

We don't mine. We reclaim. Every bracelet, pendant, and ring begins as scrap metal, broken wire, or a plastic water bottle — salvaged, refined, and reimagined into something worth wearing. Our studio produces zero mining waste and is powered by MSU students who care about their campus and planet.
